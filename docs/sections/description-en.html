PTRO models key aspects of preclinical studies, including:

  • Animal demographics
  • Treatment exposures
  • Body weight records
  • Mortality diagnoses
  • Laboratory test results
  • Clinical observations
  • Experimental procedures

The ontology was developed through a semi-systematic methodology combining:

  1. Extraction of core concepts from SEND datasets.
  2. Reuse and alignment with existing ontologies, such as the Radiation Oncology Ontology (ROO) and the NCI Thesaurus (NCIT).
  3. Iterative refinement with input from domain experts.

The Semantic Types Ontology (STY) is integrated to provide high-level categorization and ensure semantic consistency across the ontology.
